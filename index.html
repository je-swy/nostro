Meet Nostro ecosystem

The Nostro ecosystem bridges the gap between traditional finance and cryptocurrencies, offering stablecoin solutions and seamless integration for businesses. Its purpose is to enable secure, low-volatility transactions, driving broader cryptocurrency adoption and economic stability.

Cryptocurrency in real economy

Integration of cryptocurrency into the real economy, ensuring transparency, convenience, and a sustainable financial infrastructure for businesses and individual users.

  • Both Nostro Token and Nostro Coin are pegged 1:1 to the Euro
  • Payments and transactions cover both online and offline operations
  • Supports payments in fiat, other cryptocurrencies, and Nostro Tokens, tailored to the needs of businesses and individual users.
  • N-Coin serves as an investment asset with a growing market value, enhancing business capitalization and providing opportunities for profit through resale.

Monetary Policy

Nostro Ecosystem integrates cryptocurrencies into the real economy through mechanisms inspired by the Federal Reserve and U.S. Treasury. Nostro Token and Nostro Coin, pegged to the euro, serve as the ecosystem’s primary currencies, facilitating payments and regulating business turnover. While Nostro Token is created and burned upon fiat transactions, Nostro Coin is a lasting asset and an instrument of monetary policy. Decentralization sets Nostro apart, enabling multiple banks worldwide to use its blockchain for localized monetary operations in various currencies.

Global accessibility and unification

The Nostro blockchain enables businesses worldwide to integrate cryptocurrencies into the real economy, regardless of jurisdiction. Financial companies can issue their own currency pairs (e.g., USD, GBP, CAD) and implement localized monetary policies, while non-financial entities can easily join as participants or corporate clients. By meeting compliance requirements, businesses unlock full blockchain capabilities, including access to tools for fiat and crypto operations, fostering global accessibility and unifying economic ecosystems under a single platform.

Freedom of transactions

Nostro offers fiat and cryptocurrency accounts for businesses and individuals, enabling seamless local and international transactions. With support for crypto-linked and traditional payment cards, it provides flexibility, global accessibility, and full compliance with EU regulations.
